Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Overview

Relay Counsel ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit relaycounsel.com or submit information through our contact form.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact form submissions: Name, email address, company name, and message content when you reach out to us.
  • Usage data: General analytics about how visitors use the Site (pages visited, time on site), collected in aggregate and not linked to individuals.
  • Cookies: We may use minimal cookies necessary for site functionality. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

3. How We Use Your Information

Information you provide through our contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry and to determine whether an engagement is appropriate. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Confidentiality

Information you share with us through the contact form prior to the formation of an attorney-client relationship is not automatically protected by attorney-client privilege. Please do not share sensitive legal matters through the contact form — those communications are better suited for a scheduled call following an engagement agreement.

5. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your information at any time by emailing info@relaycounsel.com.

6. Third-Party Services

We use Netlify to host this Site. Netlify may collect standard server logs and form submission data on our behalf. Their privacy practices are governed by the Netlify Privacy Policy. We use Google Calendar for appointment scheduling; their practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

7. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at info@relaycounsel.com.

9. Children's Privacy

This Site is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised date. Your continued use of the Site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Reach us at info@relaycounsel.com.
