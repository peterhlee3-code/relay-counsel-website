What we handle

Transactional legal, covered end to end.

From formation to complex commercial deals, we handle the full range of transactional work your company needs as it grows.

Commercial Contracts & SaaS Agreements

MSAs, SaaS subscription agreements, order forms, and customer-facing terms — drafted on your paper and negotiated.

Employment & Equity Compensation

Offer letters, option grants, RSAs, equity plan administration, and employment policy frameworks for growing teams.

Corporate Formation & Governance

Delaware C-corp formation, cap table structuring, bylaws, board consents, and stockholder agreements done right from day one.

IP & Technology Licensing

Privacy & Regulatory Compliance

Outside Counsel Management

NDAs & Confidentiality Agreements

How it works

Pass the baton. We'll handle our leg.

Relay Counsel is built around a simple idea: you focus on building, we focus on the legal. Three steps, no handoff friction.

01

You bring the project

Tell us what you're working on — a fundraise, a new vendor contract, a hire. No intake forms, no intake calls. Just start the conversation.

02

We run our leg

We draft, negotiate, review, and advise. You get a dedicated attorney who understands your business — not a rotating team of strangers.

03

You get the baton back

Work returned complete. No open loops, no legal landmines. You keep building while we stay ready for the next leg of the race.
